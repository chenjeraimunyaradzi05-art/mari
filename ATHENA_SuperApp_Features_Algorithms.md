1. **Likes** (strength of positive engagement)

6. **Subscriptions** (follow after watching)
7. **Sounds** (trending audio drives reach)
8. **Hashtags** (topic discovery)
9. **Description** (relevance keywords)

**TikTok weights these by:**
- Neural networks analyzing patterns
- Real-time data processing
- Temporal Convolutional Networks (TCN) for sequence prediction
- Video completion rate (most important)
- First 3 seconds engagement (critical)
