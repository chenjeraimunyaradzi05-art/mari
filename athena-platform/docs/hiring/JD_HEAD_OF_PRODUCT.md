# JOB DESCRIPTION: Head of Product

**Role:** Head of Product  
**Location:** Sydney, Australia (Hybrid)  
**Equity:** 1-5%  
**Salary:** AU$140k - $160k + Super

## About ATHENA
ATHENA is a comprehensive Life Operating System for Women. We are empowering 500 million women globally to achieve financial independence.

## The Role
As Head of Product, you will be the bridge between our vision and the user's reality. You will own the product roadmap, user research, and the execution of our "persona-based" architecture.

## Responsibilities
- **Roadmap:** Define and prioritize the product backlog based on user value and business goals (MVP -> Launch -> Scale).
- **User Research:** Conduct interviews with women across our 9 target personas (Early Career, Entrepreneur, Returner, etc.).
- **Design:** Collaborate with UI/UX designers to translate requirements into high-fidelity prototypes.
- **Data:** Define success metrics (activation, retention, LTV) and set up analytics (Mixpanel/Amplitude).

## Requirements
- **Experience:** 5+ years in product management, preferably in B2C tech or EdTech/FinTech.
- **User-Centric:** Obsessive about user experience and solving real problems.
- **Analytical:** Comfortable with data, SQL skills are a plus.
- **Execution:** Proven track record of shipping products from zero to one.

## What We Offer
- Key leadership role with significant autonomy.
- Meaningful equity in a high-growth startup.
- Direct impact on the lives of women globally.
