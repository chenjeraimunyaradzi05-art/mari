   - Cost: AU$0

   - Cost: AU$100-500 (agreement drafting)
   - Complexity: Medium
   - Compliance: Moderate (partnership agreement)

   - Cost: AU$500-1,500 (ASIC registration, directors setup)
   - Complexity: Medium-high
   - Compliance: ASIC requirements, company law compliance

   - Compliance: Complex (trustee responsibilities)
